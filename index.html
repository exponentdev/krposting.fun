Welcome to Kataastaasang Resolusyon Posting (KRP), a Filipino-inspired Survival Multiplayer (SMP) Minecraft server where players immerse themselves in a world designed to replicate the rich cultural diversity and geographical beauty of the Philippines. This unique server is centered around a massive 1:40 scale model of the Philippine archipelago, representing 40,000 by 40,000 ingame blocks size.
